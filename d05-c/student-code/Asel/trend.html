BASIC PALETTE OF TRENDY COLORS

According to Pantone Color Institute experts, the colors of the new season palette reflect our relationship with color now and how they were influenced by our experience of recent years. It's also as if the palette is a celebration of new-found freedom and the excitement of waiting for something new. So the fashion colors are full of expression, bringing energy and bright colors to our wardrobes/outfits. Among which any of us can find the "most" shade that best conveys the mood.

Fiery Red

The rich red of Fiery Red is energizing. But throughout history and in different cultures red has been the color of blood, passion, the clothing of kings and nobles. And now the fiery scarlet remains both a symbol of love and a struggle for power.

Beetroot Purple

The bold shade of fuchsia Beetroot Purple symbolizes the fruits of nature. The bright Beetroot Purple is one of the most memorable color trends of summer 2023.

Tangelo

Juicy orange Tangelo, like its delicious counterpart, is rich in vitamins and vibrant in flavor.

Peach Pink

The delicate peach shade of Peach Pink invites you into a warm embrace. Combining the warmth of orange with the tenderness of pink, Peach Pink is feminine and energetic.

Empire Yellow

Empire Yellow as the color of joy, happiness and spring represents optimism and lightness of being.

Crystal Rose

Crystal Rose is a delicate pink as a symbol of modern romance. According to Pantone experts, the delicate pink in the color palette of spring and summer 2023 is a response to people's need to distance themselves from the madness of modern society and the emotional stress of recent months.

Summer Song

Summer Song by Pantone is a pure blue shade that represents tranquility and relaxation.

Blue Perennial

Pantone's muted blue Blue Perennial perfectly complements this trendy palette with its poise and versatility.

Classic Green

Classic Green is an invigorating green color rich in health benefits like fresh herbs.

THE MAIN TRENDS OF THE SEASON

Maxi

For the second year, the most popular length is maxi. But in the new season in addition to dresses and skirts, extremely long and laconic style coats in a minimalist style are striking. The discreet sexiness and elegance of the products maxi length makes them one of the most coveted items of the new season's closet for every day.

Transparent Fabrics

It's no secret that over the past few seasons the trend for femininity is in fashion. In their spring / summer collections designers once again introduced things made of semi / transparent fabrics. Therefore actively wear dresses, skirts, tops, pants, gloves and so on, showing the beauty of the female body. Light, weightless images are complemented by coarse shoes and tights. Combine with more usual things (for example: oversize jacket, knitwear, denim).

Corset

If you have not decided on this piece of closet in previous seasons, it is recommended to give a chance to corsets in the spring, because they can be worn not only for special occasions, but also in everyday life, reducing the degree of idleness and sexuality in combination with wide jeans, cargot pants / parachutes, mini and maxi skirts, knitwear, shirts, etc.

Tops and Bras

The fashion for "cropped" clothes does not give up its position. In popular social networks bloggers cut their shirts, blouses, T-shirts, dresses, jeans, jumpsuits, etc. Therefore, spring and summer season designers could not leave fashionistas without everyone's favorite cropped tops and bras. Cropped tops and bras are versatile and perfectly applicable in different styles. They can be a part of layered outfits as well as an independent item in the image.

Mini

Ultra short skirts, inspired by Miu Miu, Diesel and other brands, were one of the key trends of last season along with maxi skirts. This year designers went even further and presented mini shorts and dresses that are perfect for sultry heat and give room for imagination in combination.
